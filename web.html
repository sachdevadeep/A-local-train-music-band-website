About Us

The Local Train is a dynamic group of passionate musicians dedicated to creating soulful and electrifying music that resonates with fans around the globe. Formed in 2008, Chandigarh and based in New Delhi since 2015, the band blends elements of rock, soul, and alternative sounds, delivering unforgettable performances and timeless tracks. The band consists of Lead Guitarist - Paras Thakur, bassist - Ramit Mehra, and Drummer and percussionist - Sahil Sarin. In April 2022, the band announced that their vocalist/frontman Raman Negi has left the band.
